About me:

My name is Leandro Funes, I am from Argentina, graduated as Electronic Technician. Over the years, I specialized in 3D design using SolidWorks, which allowed me to cultivate precision, creativity, and problem-solving skills. These same qualities have now guided me into an exciting career transition toward IT universe.

I'm currently migrating from my previous field into the dynamic world of technology. As a Full Stack JavaScript Developer student at Desafío Latam, I've discovered a genuine passion for programming and web development that drives me to code every day.

My learning journey encompasses both frontend and backend technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, Express.js, and SQL. While I appreciate the visual aspects of web development, my interests extend far beyond just web design—I'm deeply fascinated by the logic, problem-solving, and architecture that programming offers.
